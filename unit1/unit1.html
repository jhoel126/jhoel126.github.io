Easy Pizza

This is a simple and easy recipe for pizza.
Many ingredients are left to personal preference.

Ingredients

  • 12" Pizza Crust
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 1 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 4 Tbsp Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Preferred Toppings
      • Some of my personal choices
    • Italian Sausage
    • Pepperoni
    • Bacon

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Begin assembling the pizza by place the pizza crust on a pan and spreading pizza sauce across the crust, leaving around 1 inch between the sauce and the edge of the crust
  3. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese to the pizza
  4. Add the preferred toppings to the pizza over the cheese
  5. Extra mozzarella cheese may be added over these toppings for a cheesier pizza
  6. Sprinkle parmesan cheese around the edge of the crust
  7. Place the pizza into the oven on the middle rack and let it cook for 18-20 minutes or until the cheese has melted and starts to brown.
